Who we are?

         We’re from Beclean, a town from Transilvania. Our school is called “Petru Rareș” National College and our mission is to empower young minds, like us, to be successful and to be creative. We’re an European Parliament Ambassador School and our school’s students participate in programs like robotic teams, debate teams, theatre teams, sport teams and in different competitions and summer camps. Our school’s motto is SAPERE AUDE, which is in latin and means Dare To Be Wise. And that exactly what we are doing every day at school: we dare to learn more. We dare to learn about each other, about ourselves, we dare to break the barriers of cultural identity and we dare to have an impact on our community in order to make our world a better place.
